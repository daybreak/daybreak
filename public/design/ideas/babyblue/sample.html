Summer is the perfect time for a change of pace - vacations, long weekends, and warm weather activities. But don't let all the fun throw you off course in your relationship with God! Chart a new course for your life by joining us for a walk through the book of 1 Peter from June into August. You'll love the journey!

A warm greeting, hot coffee and a relevant message are waiting for you. We offer a casual atmosphere where we are more concerned with meeting your needs than how you're dressed. Check out these photos. Click for picture gallery.

Music is at the heart of our weekend worship services, and our contemporary energetic sound will inspire you. Listen to a sample of our uplifting encouraging music!

Looking for practical help for real life situations you can really use? Test drive us by listening to a short segment of a recent message.
